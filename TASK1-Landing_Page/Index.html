VARIETIES

Button Mushroom

It is white and brown coloured mushroom. It is One of the most commonly and widely consumed mushroom in the world.

Cup Mushroom

This mushroom tends to grow like cup. This is usually orange coloured mushroom. Cup mushroom contains copper and potassium.

Oyster Mushroom

Oyster mushroom used to treat high cholesterol, infections, diabetes and cancer. Oyster mushroom are eaten in a variety of cuisines and are specially popular in chinese, japanese and korean food.

Paddy Straw Mushroom

Paddy Straw Mushroom contains natural insulin which is good for diabetes. It prevents Anaemia and is good for heart health.

Shiitake Mushroom

Shiitake Mushrooms are the second most commonly eaten mushroom in the world. Shiitake have smokier and more savory umami flavoor. It is mostly used in soups and stews.

Enoki Mushroom

Enoki Mushroom are the versatile, tasty fungus known for their crisp texture and mild flavor. Enoki is rich in fibre, B nd D vitamins and essential minerals such as potassium , iron and phosphorus.
